# Resume Optimizer

## Overview

You have 6 seconds before your resume is rejected or moves forward. Recruiters scan for **metric-driven accomplishments** using **power verbs** and **clear impact**. This skill transforms generic descriptions into XYZ-formula statements that survive that brutal 6-second scan and directly address job requirements.

**Core principle:** Every bullet must answer "What measurable business impact did I create?" If you can't quantify it, remove it.

> ⚠️ **File Rule:** Never overwrite or replace the original resume file. Always create a **new file** named after the target job (e.g., `resume-revolut-product-owner.tex` or `resume-stripe-engineering-manager.pdf`). The original resume is your source of truth — treat it as read-only.

## When to Use

**Use this skill when:**
- Tailoring resume to specific job posting
- Current bullets use weak verbs (helped, assisted, worked on, responsible for)
- Missing numbers/metrics on achievements
- Recruiter is likely scanning <6 seconds
- Need to emphasize skills matching job description

**Don't use when:**
- Achievements genuinely have no measurable impact (remove instead)
- Listing only job duties (those belong elsewhere—focus on achievements)

## The XYZ Formula (Core Pattern)

**Accomplished [X] as measured by [Y] by doing [Z]**

- **[X]** = Business outcome (what you delivered/improved/solved)
- **[Y]** = Quantifiable metric (%, $, time saved, users impacted, scale)
- **[Z]** = Specific action/method (the leverage point that made it happen)

### Template Structure

```
Accomplished [business outcome] as measured by [specific metric/number] by doing [concrete action].
```

### Real Examples from Your Resume

**BEFORE (Generic):**
- Guided the global Digital Marketing Transformation of an American athletic footwear company by implementing Adobe Real-Time CDP, Journey Optimizer, elevating customer engagement and driving $700M+ in incremental revenue.

**AFTER (XYZ Formula):**
- Architected global Digital Marketing Transformation leveraging Adobe Real-Time CDP & Journey Optimizer, driving **$700M+ incremental revenue** (X: revenue lift) **by unifying 40M+ customer profiles** across channels and automating 200+ journeys (Z: specific methods).

**BEFORE (Weak verb, no scale):**
- Delivered workshops to 50+ stakeholders, increasing platform adoption rate by 60% through impactful demonstrations.

**AFTER (Power verb, clear impact):**
- Architected 15 platform training workshops reaching 200+ stakeholders across 5 regions, **accelerating adoption by 60%** and **reducing support tickets by 35%** through hands-on demonstrations of ROI impact.

## Quick Reference: Power Verbs

Replace weak verbs with these:

| Weak Verb | Power Verb |
|-----------|-----------|
| Helped | Architected, Accelerated, Scaled |
| Assisted | Engineered, Optimized, Automated |
| Worked on | Launched, Drove, Delivered |
| Responsible for | Owned, Managed, Led |
| Was involved in | Spearheaded, Pioneered, Established |
| Participated | Collaborated, Partnered, Aligned |
| Did | Implemented, Deployed, Built |

## Implementation: Resume Optimization Process

### Step 1: Extract Weak Bullets
Find all bullets containing: helped, assisted, worked on, was responsible for, participated, involved, did

### Step 2: Add Missing Metrics
For each bullet, ask:
- What % improved?
- What $ generated or saved?
- How many people/scale affected?
- How much time saved?
- What was the adoption rate?

**If no metric exists:** Either find one (check job titles for KPIs, ask yourself what success looked like) or **delete the bullet** — filler kills your resume.

### Step 3: Apply XYZ Formula
Rewrite using: "Accomplished [X - business outcome] as measured by [Y - metric] by doing [Z - specific leverage point]"

### Step 4: Power Verb Check
Replace every weak verb with power verb from table above

### Step 5: Job-Specific Alignment
Scan target job description for:
- Keywords (SEO, CRO, GEO, analytics, data models, CMS, experimentation)
- Required skills (5+ years high-growth, website launch experience, stakeholder management)
- Success metrics they care about (sign-ups, CRO, data quality, user experience)

Reorder bullets to **highlight matching skills first**, add job keywords naturally into descriptions

### Step 6: Visual Hierarchy Enforcement

**Name:** Largest font, centered
**Section headers:** Bold, smaller than name
**Bullets:** Small text, 1-2 lines max per bullet
**Eye flow:** Top → bottom, left → right (no complex formatting)

### Step 7: One-Page Enforcement (or 1.5 for 5+ years experience)

**Delete in order:**
1. Outdated technologies (technologies >5 years old not in job description)
2. Jobs >10 years ago (one-liner only)
3. GPA if <3.5 or >3 years post-graduation
4. Irrelevant projects or skills
5. Employment gap explanations (formatting handles this — no dates in brief periods)
6. Generic summary statements
7. Skills not in target job description

### Step 8: Skills Section Reorganization

Reorganize skills to match job requirements:

**Target: Revolut Product Owner (SEO, CRO, GEO, data, CMS, leadership, growth)**

```
Digital Marketing & Growth: SEO, CRO/GEO, A/B Testing, Analytics, Google Analytics,
Firebase, BigQuery, Data-driven optimization, User experience

Products & Platforms: Adobe Real-Time CDP, Journey Optimizer, Adobe Analytics,
Google Tag Manager, Tealium, CMS management

Technical: SQL, Python, JavaScript, APIs, Data pipelines, Google Looker Studio, Power BI

Leadership & Strategy: Product roadmap, Cross-functional leadership, Stakeholder management,
Agile, Data-driven prioritization, Growth strategy
```

**Bad organization:** Lists everything flat without job context
**Good organization:** Highest-relevance skills first, grouped by category

### Step 9: Before/After Comparison

Create tracked-changes document showing:

| Section | Before | After | Change |
|---------|--------|-------|--------|
| Power verb | "Delivered workshops" | "Architected 15 training workshops" | Weak→Power verb, added scale |
| Metrics | "increasing adoption by 60%" | "$700M+ revenue, 60% adoption, 35% support reduction" | Added revenue & efficiency metrics |
| Formula | "through impactful demonstrations" | "...by unifying 40M+ profiles and automating 200+ journeys" | Generic→specific XYZ leverage |
| Alignment | Not mentioned | "Aligns with target: CRO, data integration, experimentation" | Added job-fit annotation |

## Common Mistakes

**❌ "Helped with" + No metrics**
- Weak verb, vague impact
- Fix: Replace with power verb + add measurable outcome

**❌ "Responsible for X"**
- Passive, no ownership signal
- Fix: "Owned X, delivering Y outcome by doing Z"

**❌ Numbers without context**
- "Managed 50K users" (so what?)
- Fix: "Scaled platform to serve 50K users, driving 30% ARPU lift by redesigning onboarding flow"

**❌ Acronyms without explanation**
- Recruiters may not know your domain acronyms
- Fix: "Adobe Real-Time CDP (customer data platform)" on first mention

**❌ Skills section full of irrelevant technologies**
- Dilutes your strongest skills
- Fix: Delete tech not in target job description; reorganize by relevance

**❌ Keeping outdated tools**
- Shows you haven't evolved
- Fix: Remove tech >5 years old unless specifically required

**❌ Summary statement that doesn't differentiate**
- "Results-oriented professional with X years..." (every resume says this)
- Fix: "Scaled digital marketing systems generating $700M+ incremental revenue through cross-functional product leadership and data-driven optimization"

**❌ One-liner jobs from 10+ years ago**
- These become resume clutter
- Fix: Remove or consolidate into timeline without detail

**❌ Gaps in employment explained on resume**
- Recruiters know careers have gaps
- Fix: Formatting handles this—no need to explain

**❌ Resume longer than one page for <10 years experience**
- Screeners automatically skip to next candidate
- Fix: Ruthlessly cut to one page

## Your 6-Second Test

**Fold your resume in half vertically.** Can a recruiter scan:
1. Name, contact (0.5 sec)
2. Summary statement - 2 sentences max (1 sec)
3. Most recent 2-3 jobs with 2-3 bullets each (3.5 sec)
4. Skills section (1 sec)

**If no,** something isn't scannable. Reformat.
